About Us

Welcome to NYAN

The National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (NYAN) is a vibrant, youth-driven organisation with an expansive national presence. It operates across all 36 states of Nigeria, including the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), with representation in the 774 Local Government Areas (LGAs) and the 109 Senatorial Districts of the nation. With more than a decade of active service, NYAN remains committed to empowering young Nigerians through initiatives that promote innovation, leadership, entrepreneurship, and good governance.

Our Core Commitments

  • Promoting youth participation in governance and policy-making processes.
  • Upholding the values of equity, freedom, justice, and civic responsibility.
  • Advocating for the integration of young people, including those with disabilities, in the implementation and monitoring of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Our Partnerships

NYAN was founded by a coalition of patriotic, energetic, and visionary Nigerian youths who firmly believe in the country's potential. Our organisation works in partnership with a wide range of stakeholders, including but not limited to:

Federal and State Ministries
National and State Assemblies
Non-Governmental Organisations
Youth Development Partners

Our Mission

To inspire and mobilise the Nigerian youth towards responsible citizenship, economic productivity, and national transformation through advocacy, training, and collaborative action.

Our Vision

To be the foremost platform for youth engagement and empowerment in Nigeria and across Africa, fostering a generation of leaders equipped to create lasting change.
